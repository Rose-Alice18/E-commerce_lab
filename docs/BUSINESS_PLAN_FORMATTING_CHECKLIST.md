## APA 7th Edition Formatting Checklist

### In-Text Citations ‚úÖ
- [ ] Author-Date format: (Smith, 2023)
- [ ] Direct quotes include page numbers: (Smith, 2023, p. 15)
- [ ] Multiple authors (2 authors): (Smith & Jones, 2023)
- [ ] Multiple authors (3+ authors): (Smith et al., 2023)
- [ ] Website citations: (Ghana FDA, 2024)
- [ ] No author: ("Title of Article", 2023)

### Reference List ‚úÖ
- [ ] Alphabetical order by author last name
- [ ] Hanging indent (0.5 inches)
- [ ] Double-spaced
- [ ] Format examples:
  - **Journal Article:**
    ```
    Author, A. A., & Author, B. B. (Year). Title of article.
    Journal Name, Volume(Issue), page-page.
    https://doi.org/xxxxx
    ```
  - **Website:**
    ```
    Author/Organization. (Year, Month Day). Title of webpage.
    Site Name. https://www.url.com
    ```
  - **Book:**
    ```
    Author, A. A. (Year). Title of book (Edition ed.).
    Publisher Name.
    ```
  - **Government Report:**
    ```
    Ghana Food and Drugs Authority. (2024). Guidelines for
    online pharmacy operations. FDA Ghana.
    ```

### Tables & Figures ‚úÖ
- [ ] Numbered consecutively (Table 1, Table 2, etc.)
- [ ] Titled above table, below figure
- [ ] Cited in text: "As shown in Table 1..."
- [ ] Source noted below: "Note. From..."
- [ ] Proper formatting (borders, alignment)

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Content Mistakes ‚ùå
- ‚ùå Vague or generic statements without evidence
- ‚ùå Unrealistic financial projections (100% growth is unlikely)
- ‚ùå Ignoring competition or risks
- ‚ùå Missing key sections from rubric
- ‚ùå Copying content from other business plans
- ‚ùå Lack of Ghana-specific context

### Formatting Mistakes ‚ùå
- ‚ùå Inconsistent fonts or font sizes
- ‚ùå Missing page numbers
- ‚ùå Incorrect margins
- ‚ùå Single-spaced when double-spaced required
- ‚ùå Poor-quality images or charts
- ‚ùå Tables that break across pages awkwardly
- ‚ùå Inconsistent heading styles

### Citation Mistakes ‚ùå
- ‚ùå Missing citations for statistics or quotes
- ‚ùå Inconsistent citation format
- ‚ùå Wikipedia as a primary source
- ‚ùå Broken or missing URLs
- ‚ùå References not matching in-text citations
- ‚ùå Using outdated sources (older than 5 years without justification)
